Project Specification

In this project, I lead a team of four to build a product from ideation to high-fidelity prototype.

How might we better understand our employees and take meaningful action to improve employee attrition? Looking at last year’s company reports, you discover the following insights:

  • Your company has lost 40,000 employees in 2021.
  • 57% of your employees say they are at least somewhat likely to quit in the next 3-6 months.
  • 45% who had quit in the past six months did so without having a new job in hand.
  • There has reported to be extremely high burnout levels during the pandemic.

Our Problem Statement

In medium-large organisations, professionals need to efficiently identify colleagues and resources best suited to assist their skill growth without having to go through multiple mediums to end up not receiving help.

Our solution should increase employee engagement and retention by allowing professionals to pursue their skill growth with guidance through a streamlined process.
