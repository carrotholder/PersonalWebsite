Project Specification

In this group assignment, we were exposed to the initial phases of the user centred design process. The outcome was a simple prototype that was to be evaluated using formal usability tests.

We were to design an interface for the overall theme of ‘COVID19’. We chose to include the following areas of focus: providing information about COVID-19, showing live stats of COVID-19 on the map and booking vaccination date and time.
